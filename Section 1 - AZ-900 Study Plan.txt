Section 1: AZ-900 Study Plan

AZ-900 : 21-Day Study Plan
If you're interested in taking the AZ-900 exam within the next 21 days, I put together this study plan that you can use to follow to reach that goal. It is attached to this lesson as a PDF resource download - scroll to the bottom of this article to download.

It aims for 30-45 minutes per day of study, and gives at least one day per week for review and doing practice tests.
